# DreamRunner First Playtest

## Session

Play for about 10 minutes without coaching. Use a browser build if possible.

## Ask Afterward

- Did movement feel satisfying and understandable?
- Was the dream signal easy to notice and follow?
- Did `Q` dimension shifting feel meaningful and readable?
- Did anything interrupt flow, feel confusing, or cause discomfort?
- Did audio feel audible, atmospheric, and balanced?
- What moment do you remember most?

## Observe

- First-click entry, mouse capture, pause/resume, and audio unlock.
- Whether jump versus held glide is understood naturally.
- Signal visibility, off-screen guidance, chunk hitches, FPS drops, and browser issues.
- Whether the player discovers dimensions without being prompted.

## Known Not Final

- Character animation, sound design, environment assets, and dimension layers are foundations.
- Passive story traces are intentionally rare.
- Preserve movement values until feedback shows a repeated problem.
